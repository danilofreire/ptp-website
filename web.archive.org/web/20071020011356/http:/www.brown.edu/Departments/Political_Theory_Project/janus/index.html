The Janus Forum


Mission

The Janus Forum is a campus initiative to encourage, facilitate, and develop active thought, debate, and discussion around political ideas at Brown. The Janus Forum seeks to actively promote critical thinking and discourse by exposing students to a variety of perspectives regarding relevant social and political issues. The Janus Forum is responsible for organizing a number of events, including:

• Large lecture events with a “Hardball Question Award” and a cash prize essay contest

• Smaller events featuring a noted Brown professors and an informal environment

• Brown Political Union Debates

To join The Janus Forum’s email list or to become more involved, email Janus@brown.edu .
