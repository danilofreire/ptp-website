Janus Forum Mission

The Janus Forum is the student arm of the Political Theory Project which seeks to inspire open-minded debate on relevant, political, social, and economic issues. Through our events, we encourage critical thinking and original discussion by exposing students to a broad range of perspectives. On this website, we invite you to access information on upcoming events, review videos and pictures from past events, or simply learn more about the Janus Forum. Additionally, do not hesitate to contact us or check the site for opportunities to get involved.

To join our email list or to contact the Janus Forum, please email: JanusForum@gmail.com
