The Janus Forum


Mission

The Janus Forum is a campus initiative to encourage, facilitate, and develop active thought, debate, and discussion around political ideas at Brown. The Janus Forum seeks to actively promote critical thinking and discourse by exposing students to a variety of perspectives regarding relevant social and political issues. Under the sponsorship of the Political Theory Project, the Janus Forum is responsible for organizing a number of events, including:

• Janus Forum Lecture Series: large lecture events with a “Hardball Question Award” and a cash prize essay contest

• Janus Conversations: smaller events featuring noted Brown professors and an informal environment

• Open Seminar Luncheons: bi-weekly lunch discussions at the Brown Faculty Club

• Brown Political Union Debates: informal public debates open to all Brown undergraduates

To join The Janus Forum’s email list or to become more involved, email Janus@brown.edu
