Should Brown favor local food?

Date: 
Thursday, November 12, 2009 - 8:00pm
Location: 
Wilson 302
Speakers: 
Grant Gilles & Johnathan Leibovic

Tomatoes shipped across the country from farms in California have a huge carbon footprint, but tomatoes from a farm up the road in Woonsocket may cost four times as much.  Which is more important for Brown Dining Services, the moral and social implications of food production or its cost?

Grant Gilles '10.5 -- Winner
