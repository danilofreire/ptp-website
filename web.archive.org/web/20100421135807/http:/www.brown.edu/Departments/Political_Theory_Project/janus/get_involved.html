Get Involved

Janus Membership

If you would like to get more involved in the Janus Forum, but you are already over-extended this program is perfect for you. If you attend any 3 Janus Forum events and sign-in on the Janus Membership computer, you will automatically become a Janus Member.  Benefits of Janus Membership include:

"If you would like to be added to our mailing list to find out about all our events please email janusforum@gmail.com"


    * A Free T-Shirt
    * Reserved seating at  Janus Lectures
    * The chance to have dinner with the speakers
    * The opportunity to give input on future lectures
    * The option to be listed on our website

Steering Committee Positions

Although the members of the Steering Committee
are selected by their own groups, there are 3 at-large representatives
elected for the academic year at the start of the Spring semester.

Student groups are also welcome to apply for a seat on the Steering Committee.  See the Steering Committee page for details.

Janus Fellows and Janus Political Union Positions

Though the Janus Fellows select their members at the start of the academic year, and the Janus Political Union selects new members at the start of every semester.  The Janus Political Union is always looking for student speakers; interested students should contact us at JanusForum (AT) Gmail (DOT) com.

Executive Board Positions

The Janus Forum Executive Board  is made up of six positions. Executive Director, Assistant Director, Marketing Director, Janus Fellows Director, Janus Political Union Director, and Steering Committee Director.

Executive Board members are elected in December for the following Spring and Fall semesters. Although prior experience within the Janus Forum is valued, it is absolutely not a prerequisite.
