Should Civic Religion Be Encouraged Or Feared?

Date: 
Friday, February 26, 2010 - 12:00pm - 1:00pm
Location: 
Faculty Club

Was the United States founded as a Christian nation? Is "civic religion" something to be encouraged, or to be feared? Is the separation of church and state an absolute good, or can it go too far?

 

Come discuss these and other issues of religion in public life at a delicious, free Faculty Club lunch this Friday. Attendance at the Thurs., Feb 25 Janus Lecture on Religion is Public Life is encouraged. Stay tuned for recommended readings.

 

We hope to see you for good food and great conversation!
