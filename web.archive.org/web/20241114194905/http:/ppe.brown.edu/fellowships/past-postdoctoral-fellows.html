Past Postdoctoral Fellows

Since 2003, the Postdoctoral Research Fellowship program has hosted more than 40 fellows across Political Science, Philosophy, Economics, and History. 

These scholars have published important scholarship in leading academic journals and books with top university presses. Such scholarly work has made significant contributions to pressing social issues. 

The aim of welcoming postdoctoral research associates to the PPE Center is to help launch their careers in an extremely competitive market. Many of our postdoctoral research associates have gone on to become rising stars in their fields, filling competitive positions at prestigious institutions. Along with their own scholarly accomplishments, former postdocs are increasingly taking prominent editorial positions in the academic community, shaping the frontiers of social science.
