Janus Political Union Debates

The  Janus Political Union  is the newest sub-group of the Janus Forum which focuses primarily on student debates.  The structure of JPU debates though is radically different from that of most other student debates. Two students have 5min each to present their arguments. Following this introduction, we split the room up in two based on people’s initial views on the topic. Then we open the debate up and audience members debate each other freely changing sides of the room during the discussion. At the end of the debate, we count up the people on each side and the introductory speaker for the winning side get to pie the other speaker.

Check out descriptions and pictures of past debates, as well as the final vote count or take a look at the upcoming debates.
