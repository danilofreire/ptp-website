Students interested in economic and philosophical aspects of freedom are encouraged to visit the following sites.



+ Institute of Economic Affairs

- The home-page of London's famous Institute of Economic Affairs. Go!

+ The Manhattan Institute.

- The Official home-page of the Manhattan Institute. Go!

+ The Ludwig von Mises Institute

- The Official home-page of The Ludwig von Mises Institute. Go!

+ A World Connected

- A wonderful resource on globalization debates. Go!

+ Institute for Humane Studies

- The home-page of The Institute for Humane Studies, with summer programs and various scholarship opportunities for students. Go!

+ The Institute for Liberty and Democracy

- The Institute for Liberty and Democracy, a Peruvian think tank headed by Hernando De Soto, has been described by former President Clinton as "the most promising anti-poverty institution in the world." Go!

+ The Mercatus Center

- The Mercatus Center, headed by Tyler Cowen, provides economic analyses connecting academic learning to real world practice. Go!

+ Institute for Justice

- Institute for Justice: A public-interest law organization which fights legal barriers to low-income entrepreneurship. Go!

+ Global Envision

- Global Envision provides cutting-edge articles on globalization from around the world. Go!

+ Center for International Private Enterprise

- Center for International Private Enterprise: promotes democratic institutions through market-oriented reform around the globe---special sections on corruption and women in business. Go!
