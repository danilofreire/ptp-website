Nov 01

PTP Postdoc Julian Mueller receives the Werner von Melle Award

Congratulations to Professor Mueller!

The Hamburg Science Foundation has awarded along with the Edmund Siemers Foundation for the third time the 10,000 Euro Werner von Melle Award. The Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the Foundation, Dr. Ekkehard Nümann, presented on October 27, the philosopher Dr. Julian F. Müller with the award at a ceremony held in the Chamber of Commerce.

Oct 13

Ben Powell of Texas Tech U. guest lectures in Prosperity

Professor Benjamin Powell of Texas Tech University and author of Out of Poverty: Sweatshops in the Global Economy with Cambridge University Press visited Brown University on October 11 to guest lecture in PTP directors' John Tomasi and Dan D'Amico's class

Oct 13

First PPE Society meetings.

The first several meetings of the inaugural PPE Society scholarship  have taken place. Click here to view the program description and schedule.

This semester's theme is inequality. Scholars have been reading material from Rousseau, Thomas Piketty, and several other sources all on the general topic of income inequality and its associated social challenges.
