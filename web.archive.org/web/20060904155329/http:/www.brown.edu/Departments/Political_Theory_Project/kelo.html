*Special Event* Debate at Brown on Supreme Court Case Kelo v. New London

The Political Theory Project is sponsoring a debate on the merits of the recent eminent domain Supreme Court Case, Kelo v. New London. The event will take place On December 5th, 2005, from 5 to 6:30 PM, in MacMillan 117. The original lawyers for both sides of the case will present oral arguments for the Brown Community. Also in attendance will be plaintiff Suzette Kelo. The debate will be moderated by Robert Flanders, former Rhode Island Supreme Court Justice

The following materials are provided in an effort to make the audience familiar with the issues pertinent to the case:


- U.S. News and World Report Article, Pre-Decision
- Background from Wikipedia (public dictionary) entry
- CNN Article with multiple points of view, Graphic slideshow on case precedents
- Supreme Court Opinions in the case of Kelo v. New London
